### Telenote



Super-fast & lightweight note app integrated with telegra.ph

Main features:

- Notes management & easy sharing
- Offline reading, search, and editing
- Auto-save and online sync
- Ready-to-type immediately after click on app icon
- Day and night mode
- Text formatting
- Fingerprint access
